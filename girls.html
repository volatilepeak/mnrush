What to Know

Season

April – July. Practices begin in March for select teams.

Grades

4th – 9th grade. Teams organized by grade and skill level.

Twice weekly, evenings, in the STMA area. High-tempo, competitive environments.

Competition

AAU events at all levels across Minnesota.

6-7 tournaments per season, primarily in-state.
