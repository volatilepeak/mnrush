What to Know
Tryout Details

Who Can Attend

Any girl entering grades 5th–10th for the 2027 school year. Open to all skill levels and schools in the area.

What to Bring

Basketball shoes, water bottle, and a good attitude. We provide the balls and pinnies.

What to Expect

Skill evaluation, competitive drills, and scrimmage play. Coaches evaluate effort, coachability, IQ, and skill.

Teams

Teams are formed by grade level. Multiple teams per grade based on turnout. Every player who registers will be placed on a team.

Season Details

Season runs April–July. Twice weekly practices in the STMA area. 6-7 AAU tournaments per season.

Questions?

Email girls@mnrush.com for any questions about tryouts or the program.
