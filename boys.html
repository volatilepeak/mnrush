Program Details
What to Know

Season

April – July. Practices begin in March for select teams.

Grades

3rd – 11th grade (9U-17U). Teams organized by grade and skill level.

Practice

Twice weekly, evenings, in the STMA area. High-tempo, competitive environments.

Competition

Prep Hoops Circuit (12U+), Prep Hoops NEXT, MYAS, and AAU events.

Tournaments

6-9 tournaments per season, primarily in-state with select out-of-state events.
